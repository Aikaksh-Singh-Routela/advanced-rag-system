Email Not Received Troubleshooting

If you don't receive the password reset email:

Step 1: Check your spam/junk folder
Step 2: Wait 5 minutes - emails can be delayed
Step 3: Verify you entered the correct email address
Step 4: Add support@example.com to your contacts and try again
Step 5: Contact support if still no email after 15 minutes

Note: Reset links expire after 30 minutes
