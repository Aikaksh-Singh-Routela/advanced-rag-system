Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the email instructions

Q: How long do refunds take?
A: Refunds process within 5-7 business days

Q: What are the password requirements?
A: Minimum 12 characters with uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters

Q: My account is locked, what do I do?
A: Wait 15 minutes after 5 failed attempts, then try again or use "Forgot Password"
